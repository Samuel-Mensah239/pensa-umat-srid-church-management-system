The Chronicles of Faith

Documenting the spiritual evolution of the PENSA UMaT-SRID Wing.

Add New Historical Milestone

1. The Divine Mandate & Inception

The history of PENSA UMaT-SRID traces back to a visionary move by the Church of Pentecost to shepherd students in higher institutions. It began as a small nucleus of spirit-filled students who refused to leave their Pentecostal fire behind at home.

2. The Era of the Pioneers

Success is built on the shoulders of giants. The Pioneer Executive Body took the mantle during a time of limited resources. They moved from hostel to hostel, room to room, evangelizing and gathering souls.

3. Intensifying the Altar Fires

As the wing gained momentum, the focus shifted to deep spiritual grooming. Weekly "Power Encounters" and "Dawn Broadcasts" became the signature of PENSA UMaT-SRID.

4. Missions and Campus Dominance

The wing's influence extended beyond the classroom. We began participating in massive "Rural Evangelism" trips, sending students to nearby villages to plant churches.

5. Technological & Digital Integration

In response to the changing times, PENSA UMaT-SRID entered its "Digital Era." We began documenting our history online and creating social media ministries.

6. Sustaining the Eternal Flame

Today, under the current leadership and the grace of the Almighty, PENSA UMaT-SRID stands as a monument of God's faithfulness.

7. Our Living Legacy: The Alumni

The story doesn't end at graduation. Our Alumni are our greatest ambassadors, serving as Christian leaders in industries across the globe.
